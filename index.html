Ballet classes at Bellanina

At Bellanina, we offer a wide selection of incredible ballet classes to dancers of all levels. You are welcome to participate whether you are a beginner, at an intermediate level or at an advanced level. We also offer coaching to those who want to take the next step and become prima ballerinas. With several years of experience teaching ballerinas, we understand the importance of great teachers. Therefore, at Bellanina, you will always have a proffessional ballet teacher at hand. Our courses are affordable in proportion to the excellent teaching you receive!

  • Intended for those who have not trained ballet before. We charge 20 £ per attended course.

  • Intended for those who have trained ballet for a while. We charge 25 £ per attended course.

  • Intended for those who have trained ballet for a long time. We charge 30 £ per attended course.
